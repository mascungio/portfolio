CASE STUDY 01

Building SOIL

Building a compostable packaging business from scratch.

CONTEXT

SOIL started as a single bioplastic material trial. Over the following years it grew into a complete compostable packaging business serving companies across Argentina.

CHALLENGE

Create a viable business around compostable packaging by simultaneously developing products, suppliers and market demand.

MY ROLE

I joined SOIL before the business formally existed and led most of its development.

I created the brand, built the supplier network, developed the first go-to-market strategy, coordinated product development, supported marketing, operated the business and shared responsibility for commercial growth.

IMPACT
100+ B2B customers
30-product portfolio
5 years of sustained growth
7.3 million plastic items replaced
KEY DECISIONS
  • Choosing the right bag supplier improved pricing, availability and product development.
  • Printing "Compostable" directly on straws created a strong commercial differentiator.
  • Switching from distributors to direct B2B sales accelerated growth.

Leading Ocean Uprise

Designing and implementing an environmental intervention.

CONTEXT

Ocean Uprise was an international program organized by Parley focused on protecting oceans through local environmental interventions.

CHALLENGE

Design and implement an environmental solution in three months, with no budget and no existing model to follow.

MY ROLE

I designed, planned and coordinated the construction and installation of a floating river barrier to capture plastic waste before it reached the Río de la Plata.

I gathered volunteers, coordinated material donations from companies and directed implementation from start to finish.

IMPACT
3 months from concept to installation
Volunteer team coordinated
Low-cost barrier developed
Field-validated prototype
KEY DECISIONS
  • Prioritized simplicity, speed and local materials to maximize feasibility.
  • Built through donations and volunteer coordination instead of waiting for funding.
